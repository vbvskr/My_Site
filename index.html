My research interest is on Numerical Linear Algbera. In particular, I wok on finding an iterative solution of a system of linear equations whose coefficient matrix can be nonsingular/singular or even rectangular using matrix splittings and generalized inverses.

Research interests:

  • Numerical linear algebra
  • Matrix Theory and Generalized Inverses
